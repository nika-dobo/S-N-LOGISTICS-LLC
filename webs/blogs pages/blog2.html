Navigating Customs: A Beginner's Guide

Navigating international customs can often feel like a maze of paperwork, regulations, and unexpected fees. However, with the right preparation, the customs clearance process can be smooth and hassle-free.

The most critical step is ensuring all documentation is accurate. This includes the commercial invoice, packing list, and bill of lading. Missing or incorrect information is the number one cause of border delays.
